# IOTB TECH Hospital – Healthcare Data Analysis Report (2019 - 2024)

## Project Overview
IOTB TECH Hospital has experienced a steady increase in patient admissions across multiple departments. To support better decision-making, healthcare records stored in Microsoft Excel were analyzed using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Excel data analysis techniques.

This report summarizes key findings from the healthcare dataset and provides meaningful business insights into:

- Patient behavior
- Revenue patterns
- Common medical conditions
- Admission trends
- Medication usage
- Insurance contributions

# Executive Summary

The analysis revealed that the hospital generates significant revenue from patient admissions and insurance providers. Medical conditions such as Arthritis, Diabetes, and Hypertension are the most common illnesses treated.

Patient admissions are distributed almost evenly across Elective, Urgent, and Emergency categories, indicating a balanced operational workload.

Medication usage is also fairly balanced, with Lipitor and Ibuprofen being the most frequently prescribed medications.

Insurance providers such as Cigna and Medicare contribute the highest hospital revenue.

# Key Insights
# 1. Patient Behavior Analysis
![Patients Analysis Table](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/easynett/IOTB-Tech-Hospital-Analysis/main/images/patients.png)

## Observations
- The hospital records **54,966 unique patient admissions**.
- The Male gender are the most admitted patients for the years under review.
- The youth of working class age between 34 and 38 are the most admitted patients.
- Patients with blood group O- are the least admitted, while A- are the most admitted indicating need for more blood donor of A- blood group.

## Insight
The hospital maintains a strong and consistent patient inflow, indicating growing trust in healthcare services and increased healthcare demand.

## Recommendation
Management should:
- Expand patient care capacity
- Improve patient scheduling systems
- Enhance patient monitoring and follow-up systems

---

# 2. Revenue Analysis

## Total Revenue Generated
- **N1.40 Billion** total billing amount generated from patient treatments.

## Average Billing Amount
- **N25,544** average billing amount per patient.

## Insight
The hospital generates strong revenue from healthcare services, indicating healthy operational performance.

Higher billing values may be associated with:
- Longer hospital stays
- Emergency admissions
- Complex medical conditions

## Recommendation
Management should:
- Monitor high-cost treatments
- Improve billing efficiency
- Track departmental profitability
- Optimize operational costs without affecting care quality

# 3. Common Medical Conditions

## Insight

Arthritis, Diabetes, and Hypertension represent the most common healthcare challenges faced by patients.

# 4. Admission Trends

## Admission Type Distribution
| Admission Type | Total Admissions |
|---|---|
| Elective | 18,473 |
| Urgent | 18,391 |
| Emergency | 18,102 |

## Yearly Admission Trend
| Year | Admissions |
|---|---|
| 2019 | 7,300 |
| 2020 | 11,172 |
| 2021 | 10,816 |
| 2022 | 10,915 |
| 2023 | 10,936 |
| 2024 | 3,827 |

## Insight
Admissions are relatively balanced across all categories.

Elective admissions are slightly higher, suggesting many patients schedule treatments in advance.

The lower admission figure for 2024 may indicate incomplete yearly data.

## Recommendation
Management should:
- Maintain adequate staffing for emergencies
- Improve elective scheduling systems
- Forecast future admission growth
- Increase hospital resource planning
